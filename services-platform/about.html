About Us

TideTrade is your go-to platform for finding trusted services near you. Whether you need personal grooming, household maintenance, or event planning, we connect you with reliable service providers in your area.

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify the process of finding and hiring professional service providers. We aim to create a seamless experience for both customers and service providers by offering a user-friendly platform where they can connect and collaborate.

Our Values

  • Trust: Building a community of trusted service providers and customers.
  • Quality: Ensuring high-quality services through verified providers.
  • Convenience: Making it easy to find and book services anytime, anywhere.
  • Innovation: Continuously improving our platform to better serve our users.
