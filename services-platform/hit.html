How It Works

For Customers

  1. Sign Up: Create an account on TideTrade.
  2. Browse Services: Explore various services offered by skilled providers.
  3. Book a Service: Select the service you need and book an appointment.
  4. Receive Service: Get the service delivered as per the booking details.
  5. Review and Rate: Provide feedback and rate the service provider.

For Service Providers

  1. Sign Up: Create an account and set up your service profile.
  2. List Your Services: Provide detailed information about the services you offer.
  3. Manage Bookings: Receive and manage booking requests from customers.
  4. Deliver Service: Provide the service as per the booking details.
  5. Get Paid: Receive payment for the services provided.
